About Us

Story

At Easygen, we understand the struggles both students and teachers
face. Students often find it hard to stay engaged while reviewing,
and teachers spend hours crafting the perfect exam. That's why
we developed Easygen—to bridge the gap between learning and
assessment with cutting-edge AI technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make learning more interactive, personalized,
and effective, while empowering teachers to save time without
sacrificing quality.
